# Privacy Policy

**SpeedHUD**

Last updated: December 19, 2025

## Introduction

SpeedHUD ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our SpeedHUD mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data

SpeedHUD requires access to your device's location services to provide core functionality:

- **Real-time GPS coordinates** — Used to calculate and display your current speed
- **Movement tracking** — Used to record trip routes, distances, and speed statistics
- **Location accuracy data** — Used to display GPS signal quality

**Important:** Location data is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit your location data to external servers.

### Trip Data

When you use the trip recording feature, the following data is stored locally on your device:

- Trip start and end times
- Route coordinates (GPS points)
- Speed measurements throughout the trip
- Distance traveled
- Average and maximum speeds

### Subscription Data

When you subscribe to SpeedHUD Pro:

- Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
- We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information
- We receive a confirmation of your subscription status from Apple

### Device Information

We may collect basic device information for app functionality:

- Device type and model (for UI optimization)
- iOS version (for compatibility)
- App version

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Display your current speed in real-time
- Record and save your trip history
- Provide HUD projection functionality
- Sync data with Apple Watch (if enabled)
- Improve app performance and user experience

## Data Storage

All your personal data, including trip history and location data, is stored:

- **Locally on your device** using iOS secure storage
- **In your personal iCloud account** (if iCloud sync is enabled)

We do not store your data on our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

- **With Apple** — For subscription management and payment processing
- **With your consent** — When you explicitly export or share your trip data

## Data Retention

- Trip data is retained on your device until you delete it
- You can delete individual trips or all history from within the app
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data stored in the app
- **Export** your trip data in GPX or CSV format
- **Delete** your trip history at any time
- **Disable** location services (note: this will disable core app functionality)

## Children's Privacy

SpeedHUD is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
